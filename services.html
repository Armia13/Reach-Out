Programs & Initiatives:

Our programs and initiatives fulfill our mission of promoting mental health, preventing mental disorders and achieving victory over mental illness through advocacy, education, research and services.

We educate

We engage the public by partnering with communities across the city to organize awareness events, and disseminate suicide prevention messaging.

We listen:

Our toll-free helpLine allows us to respond personally to hundreds of thousands of requests each year, providing free information and support—a much-needed lifeline for many.

We lead:

Public awareness events and activities, including Mental Illness Awareness Week, successfully fight stigma and encourage understanding.

We Offer:

Our credible print resources are available for purchase, online resources for those looking for help or more information.
